Woodpecker

Woodpeckers can be found almost everywhere on the planet except in extremely cold enviornemnts. They are also not found in Australia, New Zealand, or Madagascar. They like to eat suet and seeds

Grass Owl

The Grass Owl lives in swampy areas or grass plains. They grow to between 32 to 38 cm. They feed on small rodents.

Emperor Penguin

The Emperor Penguin is the largest penguin species alive. They live in Antartica. They are probably most known for the movie Happy Feet.
